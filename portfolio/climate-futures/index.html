Climate futures workshop

June 2020

climate futures is a discursive, participatory design workshop intended to provide an accessible, creative, and fun way of exploring different worldviews in the context of climate change.

  • Team: Dinah Coops, Julie T. Do, Kyle Thomas
  • Role: Researcher & designer
  • Timeline: January-June 2020
  • Methods: Subject matter expert interviews, survey, co-design, participatory design, discursive design

The cumulation of this work is the Climate futures workshop anthology and guidebook:

  • Anthology (PDF)
  • Guidebook (PDF)

For further documentation:

  • solastalgia.carrd.co (mini-site)
  • Research & process book (PDF)

This work was done as an independent capstone project as part of the Human-Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) master's program at the University of Washington in Seattle (UW).
